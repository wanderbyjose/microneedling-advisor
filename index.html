About this App

This tool was built for personal use — it reflects how I, Jose, assess and plan my own microneedling treatments. I built it to organise my thinking, track what works for my skin, and share the logic behind my routine with anyone curious about doing the same.

The science behind every recommendation — needle depths, seasonal adjustments, serum timing, cartridge choices — has been extracted from the peer-reviewed sources listed below. They are shared here for full transparency so you can verify every decision this app makes.

Product recommendations point to MeamoShop, a Seoul-based K-beauty supplier I personally use and trust for professional-grade skin boosters and microneedling serums.
